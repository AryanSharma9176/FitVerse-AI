# Product Vision

## Project Name

**FitVerse AI**

---

# Vision Statement

To build an AI-powered nutrition assistant that enables people to understand, track, and improve their eating habits effortlessly using computer vision, machine learning, and personalized nutrition intelligence.

---

# Mission Statement

FitVerse AI aims to remove the friction from traditional calorie tracking by allowing users to simply capture an image of their meal and instantly receive an intelligent nutritional analysis, helping them make healthier decisions without manual food logging.

---

# Problem Statement

Millions of people want to eat healthier and track their nutrition, but most nutrition tracking applications require tedious manual input.

Users often need to:

- Search for each food item
- Estimate serving sizes
- Select the correct food from long lists
- Log meals multiple times a day

This repetitive process leads to poor user engagement and causes many users to abandon nutrition tracking altogether.

---

# Proposed Solution

FitVerse AI uses Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision to simplify nutrition tracking.

Instead of manually logging meals, users can capture a food image, allowing the AI system to:

- Detect multiple food items
- Estimate portion sizes
- Calculate calories
- Analyze macronutrients
- Provide nutritional insights
- Track eating habits over time

The goal is to make nutrition tracking as simple as taking a photograph.

# Core Features

## AI Food Recognition

Automatically identify food items from an uploaded image.

---

## Portion Size Estimation

Estimate approximate serving sizes for each detected food item.

---

## Nutrition Analysis

Calculate:

- Calories
- Protein
- Carbohydrates
- Fat
- Fiber (future enhancement)

---

## Daily Nutrition Tracking

Maintain daily meal history and nutritional summaries.

---

## Personalized Goals

Allow users to configure:

- Weight loss
- Weight maintenance
- Muscle gain

The application will provide personalized calorie recommendations based on user goals.

---

## AI Nutrition Assistant

Provide intelligent insights such as:

- Daily nutritional balance
- Meal quality assessment
- Suggestions for improvement
